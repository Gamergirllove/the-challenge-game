How to Play

Every Round Is a Fight for Survival

Each game cycle puts your reflexes, strategy, and social skills to the test. Score high, dodge the vote, win the arena — or go home.

01

Daily Mini-Game

All players compete simultaneously. Top scorers earn safety. The bottom player faces elimination risk.

02

The Discussion

Chat with rivals. Form alliances. Drop hints or misdirect. 60 seconds before the vote locks in.

03

The Vote

Players vote for who they want to face in the arena. The two most-voted players go head-to-head.

04

Arena Duel

A head-to-head battle in a harder game. Loser is eliminated. Winner earns a Career Win.

Features

Built Different

Hall of Champions

A persistent global leaderboard tracks career wins. Earn your spot and defend it.
